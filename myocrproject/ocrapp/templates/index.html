OCR Project FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our OCR project, from how it works to the languages it supports and how you can integrate it into your workflows.

1. What is OCR and how does it work?

OCR, or Optical Character Recognition, is a technology that converts different types of documents, such as scanned paper documents, PDF files or images captured by a digital camera, into editable and searchable data. Our OCR solution employs deep learning algorithms to enhance accuracy and speed.

2. Which languages does your OCR support?

Our OCR technology supports over 80 languages, including widely used languages such as English, Chinese, Spanish, and French, as well as less common languages and scripts. This makes our solution ideal for global applications requiring multilingual support.

3. How can I integrate your OCR into my application?

Our OCR can be easily integrated into your applications through our API. The API is designed to be straightforward to use, allowing you to quickly add OCR capabilities to your software or services. Documentation and code samples are available to get you started.

4. What formats can the OCR output?

Our OCR technology supports multiple output formats, including json, and jpg. This versatility ensures that the extracted data can be easily used or further processed according to your specific needs.
